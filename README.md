# Ballot Right

Ballot right is a cutting-edge voting application designed to streamline the electoral process. It offers a secure, user-friendly platform for casting votes, ensuring transparency and integrity in elections. With features like real-time results tracking, voter authentication, and an intuitive interface, Ballot Right aims to make voting accessible and efficient for everyone. Whether for local elections, organizational votes, or national polls, Ballot Right is your trusted partner in democracy

## Background

In today's fast-paced digital world, traditional voting methods can be cumbersome and prone to errors. Ballot Right was inspired to solve the error in voting in school settings by providing a reliable and efficient online voting solution. Our mission is to empower voters with a seamless experience while upholding the highest standards of security and transparency.

## Features

- **Secure voter authentication**: Ensures that only eligible voters can participate, protecting the integrity of the election.
- **Real-time results tracking**: Provides instant updates on vote counts, allowing for transparency throughout the voting process.
- **User-friendly interface**: Designed for ease of use, making it simple for voters of all ages and tech-savviness to cast their ballots.
- **Google Authentication**: Integrates with Google for secure and convenient voter login.
- **Responsive design**: Accessible on various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
